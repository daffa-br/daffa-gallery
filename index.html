Welcome to Daffa Gallery and a Piece of Memory in the Quiet of Semarang

In the quiet, time-forgotten streets of Semarang, there exists a cherished piece of memory, nestled amidst the city's colonial heritage. The Old Town of Semarang, with its charming Dutch architecture and narrow lanes, holds within its weathered walls the echoes of a bygone era.

About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Daffa Zamiira Pradana. I am a Chemical Engineering student of Semarang State University. I was born and raised in Semarang. I have hobbies in photography and editing.
